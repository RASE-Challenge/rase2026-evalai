Submissions will be evaluated using both objective and perceptual metrics to ensure fair and holistic assessment.

  • PESQ (Perceptual Evaluation of Speech Quality): Measures perceptual quality of enhanced speech compared to clean reference.
  • ESTOI (Extended Short-Time Objective Intelligibility): Estimates intelligibility of speech in noisy environments.
  • DNSMOS: A neural networkâ€“based non-intrusive measure of speech naturalness and quality.
  • MFCC Similarity: Compares mel-frequency cepstral coefficients between enhanced and reference signals to capture spectral fidelity.

A final weighted composite score will be calculated by combining all metrics. Leaderboards will be ranked based on this composite score. Baseline models and their performance will be provided for reference.
